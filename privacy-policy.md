# Pherus

> **"A living, breathing hub where folks from all walks crash together."**

Pherus is not a slick corporation. It is a collective, a research house, and an open-source practical repository. While legally registered as **Pherus SMC LTD** in Uganda, its soul is that of a "house" or "organization"—a fortress of solitude for those building something real. 

We aim to pioneer in technology, research, and applied sciences. We aren't there yet, and we aren't pretending to be. We are building.

---

## 🏛 The Structure: The Houses
Pherus operates through internal factions—**"Houses"**—where ideas, engineering, and philosophy intersect:
* **House of La:** The core of our research, wild concepts, and the deep philosophy behind the "Big Whys."
* **Engineering Factions:** The builders tinkering in the corners, turning abstract theory into functional reality.
* **Art & History Factions:** Documenting the human experience and the aesthetics of our wildest dreams.

This is a ragtag crew aiming to fix the messes the world pretends aren't there. When one leader falls, another from the collective is selected. The story continues.

---

## 🧭 Our Eight Pillars
We focus on the foundations of human dignity and the "underbelly of the universe."

1.  **Digital Healthcare Access:** Breaking borders and corporate gatekeeping. Any doctor, any medication, anywhere.
2.  **Trans Care & Human Rights:** Healthcare designed with dignity at its core—not as an afterthought, but as a foundation.
3.  **Aerospace & Advanced Technology:** Ambitious leaps and cutting-edge tech that provide genuine impact, not vanity projects.
4.  **Housing & Homelessness Solutions:** Restoring autonomy and hope through dignified housing, because shelter is a human right.
5.  **Economic Justice & Resource Access:** Flipping the script on systemic poverty by making education and resources accessible to those locked out by design.
6.  **Medical & Prosthetic Innovation:** Advanced prosthetics at half the market cost. Inclusive innovation for women, trans individuals, and children.
7.  **Research & Epistemology:** Questioning how we know what we know. Building knowledge systems that serve humanity over ideology.
8.  **Community & Collaboration:** A global network of philomaths and changemakers crashing together to build something real.

## 📈 The Mission Timeline
